About

Hi, I'm Toni — the designer and creator behind TGFirelight.

I'm a digital content and physical product creator working at the intersection of design software and the workshop. Everything starts as a vector file — then some of it stays digital, and some of it goes through the laser cutter or the 3D printer to become something you can hold.

My Etsy shop only offers digital products (for now): ready-to-cut SVG and DXF files or/and PNG files for engraving for makers with their own machines and other digital products that you can check out in store.

  • .svg / .dxf

    Digital cut files

    Ornaments, medal holders, decorative envelopes, and more — ready for your laser cutter.

  • .png

    Laser engraving files

    Highly detailed / Highly contrast files ready for engraving

  • pla / petg

    3D printed pieces

    Small functional and decorative objects, printed and finished in-house.

  • .pdf

    Kids' storybooks

    Short, illustrated PDF picture books for young children.

  • info

    Product information

    Each product contains an information file that explains all the steps needed to create it.
